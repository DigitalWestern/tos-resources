# TOS Resources

A static website hosting community-driven resources for understanding, diagnosing, and managing Thoracic Outlet Syndrome (TOS). All materials are compiled from insights gathered across top Reddit posts and discussions.

**Live site:** [tosresources.com](https://tosresources.com)

## Resources

### Reports & Guides
- **Community Research Report** — A comprehensive summary of experiences, treatments, and advice shared by the TOS community on Reddit.
- **Surgical Outcomes** — Community-reported surgical outcomes, recovery timelines, and key considerations.
- **Diagnostic Checklist** — A step-by-step checklist to help guide conversations with your doctor and navigate the TOS diagnostic process.

### Exercise Trackers
- **12-Week Exercise Tracker** — A structured 12-week exercise program compiled from the most recommended routines and exercises for TOS relief.
- **12-Week Exercise Tracker (No Nerve Flossing)** — The same program with all nerve flossing exercises removed.

## Additional Resources

For more information on Thoracic Outlet Syndrome, visit [tosoutreach.com](https://www.tosoutreach.com).

## Disclaimer

The information provided on this website and in the downloadable files is for general informational and educational purposes only. It is not intended as, and should not be used as, a substitute for professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. The content is compiled from community discussions on Reddit and has not been reviewed or verified by medical professionals.
