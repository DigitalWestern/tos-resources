Community & Resources

External tools, support communities, and educational resources curated for people navigating Thoracic Outlet Syndrome.

Find a Surgeon

Finding an experienced TOS surgeon is one of the most consequential decisions you'll make. These resources help you identify specialists with documented community experience.

Web
TOS Outreach — Surgeon Finder Community-compiled directory of TOS surgeons organized by region, with notes on specialization and surgical approach
